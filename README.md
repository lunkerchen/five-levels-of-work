</p>

**Core thesis:** Level 4 is the baseline expectation; Level 5 is the goal.

<table>
<tr><td><b>L1 — Spot</b></td><td>「There's a problem.」 — Pure notification. Pushes thinking upward.</td></tr>
<tr><td><b>L2 — Diagnose</b></td><td>「There's a problem, I found some causes.」 — Adds analysis but no path forward.</td></tr>
<tr><td><b>L3 — Options</b></td><td>「Here's the problem, causes, and possible solutions.」 — Presents choices without conviction.</td></tr>
<tr><td><b>L4 — Recommend</b></td><td>「Here's the problem, root cause, and my recommendation.」 — Diagnosis with a clear call.</td></tr>
<tr><td><b>L5 — Execute</b></td><td>「I identified it, fixed it, keeping you in the loop.」 — Autonomous resolution.</td></tr>
</table>

---

## Why

Most people operate at L1-L3 — they identify problems but push the thinking work upward. High agency means you own the full chain: spot → diagnose → decide → execute. Only the final summary reaches the decision-maker.

This framework gives everyone a shared vocabulary for what "good" looks like.

---

## How to Use

- **As a manager:** Tell new hires "Live at L4 from day one. As trust builds, rise to L5."
- **As an IC:** Before escalating, ask "What level am I bringing?"
- **As an AI agent user:** Structure requests with diagnosis + recommendation (minimum L3). Expect L5 delivery from your agent.
- **As a reviewer:** L1-L2 → return for context. L4-L5 → brief acknowledgment.

# 五層工作等級

<p align="center">
  高主動性團隊與 AI Agent 的決策協作框架。
</p>

**核心命題：L4 是門檻，L5 是目標。**

<table>
<tr><td><b>L1 通報</b></td><td>「有問題。」— 純通報，把思考外包給上級</td></tr>
<tr><td><b>L2 診斷</b></td><td>「有問題，我找到一些原因。」— 有分析但沒方向</td></tr>
<tr><td><b>L3 選項</b></td><td>「問題是 X，原因可能是 A/B，解法有 C/D。」— 有選項但沒立場</td></tr>
<tr><td><b>L4 推薦</b></td><td>「問題是 X，我認為主因是 A，建議解法 C。」— 診斷+明確建議</td></tr>
<tr><td><b>L5 執行</b></td><td>「我發現→診斷→修復→順便跟你說一聲。」— 自主解決+知會</td></tr>
</table>

---

## 為什麼

多數人停留在 L1-L3 — 發現問題但把思考成本外包給上級。High agency 代表你擁有完整鏈條：發現→診斷→決定→執行。只有最終摘要抵達決策者。

這個框架讓團隊對「什麼叫做好」有共同語言。

---

## 怎麼用

- **管理者：** 跟新人說「從第一天起活在 L4，隨著信任累積上升到 L5。」
- **執行者：** 升級問題前先問自己「我帶的是哪一級？」
- **AI Agent 使用者：** 任務指令至少 L3（問題+原因+方案方向）。要求回報永遠 L5。
- **審查者：** L1-L2 退回補 context。L4-L5 只需確認。
